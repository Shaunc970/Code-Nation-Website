Describe further improvements you can make to your project

Improvements I could of made to my project if time was not a factor and the functionality completed would of been:
The main issue with the website currently that I would of worked on if there was no time constraints would of the been the visual and user interface side of the project, I would of done more things such as better utilisation of images and overall just made the site more pleasant to look at and interact with this could of included things such as the use of buttons on the site rather then text links. I would have also made a number of accessibility changes such as the use of different colour schemes and themes on the website both for people who are colour blind and if users want a darker theme for less eye strain while interacting with the site. These changes would also of included alt descriptions for partially sighted users so their narrator software can read descriptions of the content.
